Dilemma Solution: Grey Hat

Brief on Grey Hat Hacking

Grey Hat Hackers use their ability as they feel best benefits the largest amount of people. If that means finding holes in systems and publishing the details of the hole that's what they will do. A Grey Hat Hacker's intention is neither good or evil, but rather a little bit of both.

This Situation

You've found a hole into a major computer organization's user database, it contains all of the information on their users in plain text. As a Grey Hat Hacker you realize this is a major hole that is most likely not only affecting this one major corporations. This is a major hole that could harm many innocent people if not handled correctly. To do what is best for the most amount of people you decide to release details about the bug, but not how you have found it or how you had done it, so that the wider community knows this exists and must be fixed as soon as possible.
