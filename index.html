Ethical Dilemma: Grey-Hat Hacking

Abstract

Consider this, you've found a backdoor into a large computer company's user database. It contains all the information they've collected on, and from,their users in plain, readable, text. What do you do with this new-found ability and information?

Possible Solutions:

  • The White Hat Way: Inform the company of your findings and do nothing with the information.
  • The Grey Hat Way: Post your findings publicly, without revealing any damning details of the backdoor or how you found it.
  • The Black Hat Way: Inform the greater community of your findings and do malicious things with the information.
